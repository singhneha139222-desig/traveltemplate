FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to visit Mount Bromo?

The best time to visit is during the dry season, from April to October. The weather is clearer, and you'll have better chances of seeing the stunning sunrise.

How do I book a tour?

You can book directly through our website by filling out the booking form, or contact us via email or phone. We'll respond within 24 hours.

What's your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before your scheduled trip. After that, a 50% cancellation fee applies.

Do you offer private tours?

Yes! We offer both group and private tours. Private tours can be customized to your preferences. Contact us for pricing.
